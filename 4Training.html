Our Procedures

Our Health Procedures:

Our staff consists of engineers, medical workers and specialized astronaut employees to help our guests stay healthy and our Hotel running smoothly and safely. We provide workout trainers to all of the guests at our hotel to keep everyone healthy and stable during their trip and stay at the Stellar Hotel.

Our Emergency Procedures:

During an emergency, stay calm. Our staff is trained to handle any and all emergencies. There is nothing to worry aobut during an emergency situation as long as everyone remains calm, collected and follows directions.

Our Emergency Launch Procedures:

Incase of an emergency during launch the launch escape system (LES) will activate. The LES consists of rockets attached to the crew capsule on the rocket. Incase of emergency all engines will cut and the capsule will detach while the LES activates. This will launch the capsule a safe distance away from the rocket before deploying parachutes.

Station Emergency Procedures:

Incase of an air leak a technician will attempt to find and seal the leak. While the technician is working you will be asked to enter the capsule, If the leak is deemed too extreme and you will be taken back to the surface immediately.

In case an object is detected and is deemed to have an imminent impact you will also be asked to enter the capsule and you will be taken back to the surface immediately.

Incase of other emergencies similar procedures will be executed.
